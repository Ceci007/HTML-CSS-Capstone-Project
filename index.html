Academy Courses works in 4 simple steps

Search

It's simple on Academy homepage, enter the name of the course you are looking for and click on search. Alternatively click on the search link on the top menu to view the list of all courses.

Locate

Get to see where the bootcamp is located by clicking on the course profile. I'ts even more exciting, you can get the direction from anywhere in the country.

Apply

Apply for your child's interview to the bootcamp or other courses of your choice. Click on the apply button to get the applycation form. We also follow up on the application to make sure you get a reply on time.

Relax

Once you get admitted. You can now relax. You can check out our blog to see what we recomend when the classes begin.
